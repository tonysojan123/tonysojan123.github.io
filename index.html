Clarify AI: Your Guide to Better Buying

Clarify is an AI-powered browser extension designed to solve one of the most pervasive problems in modern online shopping: decision overload. As the digital commerce ecosystem grows more fragmented and saturated with unreliable reviews, sponsored content, and inconsistent product information, consumers increasingly struggle to make confident purchase decisions.

Clarify provides a simple but powerful solution: an AI-powered browser extension that consolidates product research into a single, accessible view. Instead of requiring users to jump between multiple tabs and platforms, Clarify analyzes information behind the scenes and then presents a clean summary directly on the product page.
